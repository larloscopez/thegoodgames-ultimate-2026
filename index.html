When

Sunday, June 28. First pull is at 10:00 AM, wrapping up around 2:00 PM. Plan to arrive by 9:45 to check in.

Can't wait until Sunday? Drop by on Saturday, where we'll be running a learn-to-play clinic and fun disc games all day.

Format & Schedule

A hat tournament means you sign up solo and we draft everyone into three balanced teams: Mango, Lemon, and Guava. The morning is a round robin where each team plays the other two, and those standings seed an afternoon final. The third-place team splits across the two finalists, so everyone plays the final and no one watches from the sideline.

5v5

Round Robin

Morning games are 5 on 5 with regular subbing. Lines will likely run 3 male-matching and 2 female-matching.

6v6

The Final

Teams combine and scale up to 6 on 6. Lines will likely run 4 male-matching and 2 female-matching.

Exact line sizes and gender ratios may shift slightly depending on signup numbers on the day.
