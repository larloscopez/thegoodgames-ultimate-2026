How It Works

A hat tournament means you sign up as an individual and we sort everyone into balanced teams, so you might be lining up with familiar faces or meeting new ones. Here's the shape of the day.

3

Three Teams

Everyone is drafted onto one of three squads (Mango, Lemon, and Guava), balanced for skill and experience so the games stay competitive and fun.

RR

Round Robin

Each team plays the other two in the morning. Win, lose, or draw, every game counts toward seeding for the afternoon final.

★

Everyone Plays the Final

The third-place team is split across the two finalists, so no one watches from the sideline. We finish the day all on the field together.

We'll run smaller lines through the round robin and scale up for the final once teams combine. Expect a healthy mix of playing time and rest, plus a few surprises to keep the day spirited.

Where to Find Us

We'll be set up at the University of Guelph Rugby / Varsity Field, corner of East Ring Road and College Road. Look for The Good Games festival signage.

Who's In

The roster so far. Teams get drafted on game day. For now, here's the crew lining up to play.

    The Details

    1

    Check In By 9:45

    Arrive a few minutes early to find your team and warm up. First pull is at 10:00 AM sharp.

    2

    Round Robin Lines

    Morning games run smaller lines so everyone gets plenty of touches, with regular subbing so you stay fresh.

    3

    Bigger Final

    Once teams combine for the final, we scale up the lines for a fuller, faster game to close out the day.

    4

    Seeding The Final

    Round robin standings decide the top two. Wins matter, but so does point differential, so keep playing hard.

    Expect the Unexpected

    We've got a twist planned that levels the playing field and rewards creativity over pure athleticism. Newer players will have their moments to shine. Come ready to play, laugh, and roll with whatever the day throws at you. You'll see.
